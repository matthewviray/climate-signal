Why disappearing Arctic ice matters

Albedo feedback: Sea ice reflects much more sunlight than dark ocean, so melting ice accelerates warming. Ocean circulation: freshwater from ice melt can disrupt large-scale circulation. Ecosystems: polar food webs depend on sea ice for hunting, breeding, and survival.

Write Up

What have we Done so far

Currently we've decided the focus of our narrative and how it will flow to mainly stress and explain the inequalities climate change has on the different parts of the regions and what can be done in the future. Especially brainstorming how to do the last part of our narrative where it will ask them to choose their country or any other country to show how their country will be impacted in the future with the different emission scenarios. We have also created the choropleth map and implemented how our features will be displayed within our prototype which includes three of our variables we will use for our final version of this project that shows the effect of climate change and global warming in different ways than just temperature rising. The variable's temperature anomaly, precipitation anomaly, and sea ice concentration help show how different regions in the world are impacted by global warming and climate change.

We also have decided to add a filter of the different emission scenarios and a decade slider to compare different decades and understand how different emission scenarios impact the future. Lastly, we have a line plot to represent the change and trend of our different variables and emission scenarios over time. As we want to emphasize the drastic effect of climate change and it's warming on the many climate variables and the world.

The most Challenging part of our project

Overall we think that making the narrative through scrollytelling will be the challenging part of the project. Specifically on deciding on what to display with the narrative text on the side for the different chapters. As we want to ensure the scrollytelling explains effectively and emphasizes climate change, chain of compounding effects and its inequalities towards different parts of the world.

Another challenging decision is figuring out what filters and features to apply towards our exploratory section in our web page. We don't want to add unnecessary filters or features that don't add much or correlate with our main topic.
